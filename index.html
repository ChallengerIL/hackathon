First steps

what is aliyah?

The Hebrew word “Aliyah” literally means ascent or rise, but for generations it has been used to mean “immigration to Israel.” Israel has always been the center of the Jewish universe, but for centuries the dream of moving to Israel was just that, a dream.

In the 1880s, organized groups began to immigrate to Israel with the goal of building communities and settling the land. “Aliyah” became the official term for immigration to Israel when, in 1950, the State of Israel opened her doors to the Jews of the world and passed the Law of Return.

Before flight

what to take care of before your flight
Before repatriation, you need to check your tax debts and pay fines and penalties.
It will be helpful to get a certificate from the insurance company for the last three years that you have not had an accident.
At your first visit to your family doctor, you will be asked to share your medical history. If you manage to save all the documents regarding your health, it is very useful to take them with you. Especially if you have health problems and there are special doctor's prescriptions.
Repatriation to Israel does not mean burning all bridges. It is not always possible to complete all cases in the country of origin. We highly recommend that you leave a general power of attorney for a loved one so that, if necessary, he has the right to resolve issues on your behalf.
All people have different needs. However, we can recommend a basic list:

  • Medicines for the first time after repatriation (1-2 months)
  • Clothes and shoes for six months ahead
  • Equipment that is much cheaper in the country of origin
  • Fleece clothing and warm slippers for a chilly Israeli winter
it is important to understand where you will enter upon arrival in Israel for the first time until you can rent permanent housing. Many choose apartments on Airbnb

At the airport

what to take care of before your flight
Certificate of new immigrant. One is issued for the whole family. Appropriate marks are put in it when using such benefits as a new immigrant, such as buying a car at a discount or duty-free import of property. Each time you contact the branch of the Ministry of Aliyah and Integration, you must have it with you.
You will be given a portion of the first payment of the absorption basket in a cash envelope. The rest of this payment will go to the bank account that you have to open.
With this form, you must come to the nearest branch of the selected health insurance fund. It is better to decide in advance in which health insurance fund you want to be served.
Before repatriation, you must check your tax debts and pay fines and penalties
You will be asked in which city you will live, and based on this, you will be given the details of your new curator at the Ministry of Aliyah and Integration.
The number is registered with the Ministry of Aliyah and Integration, you can transfer it to yourself at the end of the validity period or simply buy a new one from any operator in the country. The availability of the Internet and the amount of minutes for communication must be clarified at the airport.

First steps in Israel

here are your next steps after making Aliyah
You need to choose the bank where you will open the first account and come to any branch closest to you. When you open an account, you will receive a credit card and a checkbook, which is necessary for renting an apartment.
Make an appointment with your curator at the Ministry of Aliyah and Integration by phone or in person. On the first visit, you must provide bank details for transferring the absorption basket. It is better to immediately clarify all the questions that have arisen by that time. If you have a licensed profession, immediately find out about possible courses, ask the curator for the contacts of the relevant ministry to obtain a license.
You need to visit the selected health insurance fund in order to receive a client card. You may be offered to upgrade to extended insurance. This is most often worth doing because new immigrants have the benefit of not having to wait for extended insurance coverage to kick in.
The "housing problem" plays a very important role in our life. This issue is especially acute immediately after repatriation to Israel, when you need to find your first home. To sign a rental agreement in Israel, you will need a checkbook, a teudat zehut, and possibly a guarantor.
As a new immigrant, you are entitled to a discount on arnona for the first 12 months. You can activate this discount for the first or second year from the date of repatriation to Israel.
If you are attending one of the programs of the Sokhnut agency, then you probably know which ulpan you need to enroll in. If you are not traveling under the repatriation program, you will be given a referral to the ulpan at the Ministry of Aliyah and Integration. The registration procedure is very simple. Come to the ulpan, find the administrative office, sign up.
Within a year from the date of repatriation to Israel, you have the right to drive a car with a driver's license issued by the country of origin. After this period or earlier, you can exchange a driver's license for an Israeli model (if the driving experience is more than 5 years) simply by appearing at the Ministry of Transport with teudat zeut, teudat ole, the rights of the country of origin (you need to make their notarized translation into Hebrew), a green form ( tofes is bright), and also by paying a fee.
Like an ulpan. Find a kindergarten or school that suits you, come during working hours and enroll your child to study. The procedure is simple.
